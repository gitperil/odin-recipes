Alfredo Sauce

Easy Creamy Alfredo Sauce

This recipe is easy to follow and only has few ingredients! Get ready to create your own delicious alfredo sauce.

Things you'll need:

  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1.5 Cups Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Clove Crushed Garlic
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Parsley

Steps:

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a medium sauce pan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cheese and garlic. Whisk continuously until heated through, stir in parsely.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
